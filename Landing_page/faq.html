GloboSense – Frequently Asked Questions

What is GloboSense?

GloboSense is a platform that connects users with professional human interpreters for real-time translations, including spoken and sign languages.

Which devices are supported?

Our app is available on iOS and macOS, with plans for Android and web versions in the future.

How can I book an interpreter?

You can search for interpreters in the app, select your preferred language pair, and schedule a call directly through the platform.

Do you support sign language interpretation?

Yes! GloboSense offers certified sign language interpreters for a variety of regional sign languages.

How do I contact support?

You can reach us via the in-app support section or email us at support@globosense.com.
