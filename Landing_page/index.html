About GloboSense – building the standard for online interpreting.

We connect companies, interpreters and agencies in one scalable ecosystem. We develop our product based on data, user feedback and the global online meetings market.

The problem we solve

Business operates globally, but interpretation is still organized manually. GloboSense allows clients to connect with the right interpreter in seconds – without emails, spreadsheets or long phone chains.

Growth model

We focus on supporting companies that run international sales meetings, customer support and technical assistance. We scale as the number of calls grows.

Market potential

The online meetings market continues to grow, and the need for real-time interpretation is becoming a standard. GloboSense positions itself as the language layer for global communication.
